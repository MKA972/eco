What is EcoPath?

EcoPath AI-powered sustainability platform that helps people track energy & gas usage, scan waste with AI, and earn rewards by completing eco-activities and quizzes — available on mobile & web.

Key Features

Eco Behaviour Tracking

Tracks user eco-behaviors: energy use (electricity & gas via API), trash scanning/sorting, daily recycling actions, and carbon footprint insights.

Eco MarketPlace

Redeem earned eco-points for environmentally friendly products, including certified reusable items and sustainable waste-management supplies.

Gamified Progress

Earn eco-XP by completing quizzes, scanning trash, and joining events — unlock levels, badges, and daily eco-quests.

Level Up Your Eco Journey

Track your recycling progress, earn achievements, and stay connected with your city’s sustainability goals — all through the EcoPath mobile app.
